Placement

Institution has placement cells which provides information, guidance and support for placement in reputed IT Companies and conducts regular training, written tests, group discussion and pre placement advisory services to enhance efficiency for attending interviews. It acts as a guiding light for building a successful carrier and future by connecting the students to the reputed Companies. Resume and interviewing assistance are available from our Career Services department and regularly hosts career fairs to help the students to get a jump start in I.T. career.

Our placement cells act as bridge between B.C.A Students and interact with reputed IT Companies in order to identify their requirements and mould the students to meet the industry standards.

Internship Program

Our college is committed to bridging the gap between academic learning and practical experience through its robust internship program. Recognizing the importance of hands-on experience in shaping successful careers, we have established strong partnerships with leading industries, organizations, and research institutions. These partnerships provide our students with diverse and enriching internship opportunities across various fields of study. Through these internships, students are able to apply their theoretical knowledge to real-world challenges, gain valuable insights into their chosen professions, and develop critical skills such as teamwork, problem-solving, and professional communication.

Our dedicated Career Services team works closely with students to identify suitable internship placements, provide resume-building workshops, and offer interview preparation sessions. By integrating internships into our academic curriculum, we ensure that our graduates are not only academically proficient but also industry-ready, equipped with the experience and confidence to excel in their careers.
