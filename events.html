Freshers

Our college conducts Freshers party every year to welcome new Students.This celebration serves as a warm welcome, helping newcomers integrate into the college community and forge new friendships. Typically organized by senior students or the college administration, the party is a blend of music, dance, games, and engaging activities designed to break the ice and create a festive atmosphere. Freshers get the opportunity to showcase their talents in various performances, participate in fun contests, and enjoy a night of entertainment and camaraderie. This event not only alleviates the initial anxiety of stepping into a new environment but also sets a positive and energetic tone for the rest of the academic year.

    The convocation program at our college is a prestigious event celebrating the academic achievements of our graduating students. The ceremony includes formal proceedings such as the conferral of degrees, inspiring speeches from esteemed guests and faculty, and addresses by selected student representatives. Graduates, adorned in traditional caps and gowns, receive their diplomas on stage, symbolizing the culmination of their hard work and dedication. The event is attended by family, friends, and members of the college community, creating a joyous and memorable atmosphere.
